Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

What types of services do you offer?

We offer comprehensive academic writing services (research papers, dissertations, editing) and technical development services (web/mobile development, AI/ML, data science, cybersecurity, IoT, blockchain, and more). Our expertise covers over 50 specialized service categories.

Do you work with emerging technologies?

Yes! We specialize in cutting-edge technologies including AI/ML (TensorFlow, PyTorch), computer vision, NLP, IoT projects, blockchain and smart contracts, cloud computing, automation, and AI chatbot development.

How long does it take to complete a project?

Project timelines vary based on complexity and scope. Simple projects (editing, small apps) take 3-7 days, research papers 5-10 days, dissertations 4-8 weeks, and complex development projects 2-12 weeks. We always provide realistic timelines during consultation.

What programming languages and tools do you work with?

We work with Python, JavaScript, Java, Kotlin, Swift, C++, R, PHP, and more. Tools include React, Angular, Vue, Node.js, Django, Flask, TensorFlow, PyTorch, MongoDB, PostgreSQL, AWS, Azure, Docker, Kubernetes, and many others.

Do you offer revisions and post-delivery support?

Absolutely! We offer unlimited revisions within the agreed scope until you're completely satisfied. For technical projects, we also provide post-delivery support, maintenance, bug fixes, and documentation to ensure long-term success.

Is my information and project kept confidential?

Yes, we maintain strict confidentiality for all client information and projects. We sign NDAs when required, never share your data with third parties, and use secure communication channels. Your intellectual property is fully protected.
